EGG FRIED RICE

Ingredients

  • Rice (As much as you want!)
  • Half an onion (Or more, depends on you)
  • A tin of sweetcorn
  • A few mushrooms
  • 1 to 2 eggs

Methods

  1. Start cooking the rice however you do, and put some oil in a frying pan.
  2. Chop the onion into bits, any size really, doesnt matter particularly and throw into the frying pan.
  3. Chop the muschrooms and add to the onions.
  4. Once the rice is cooked, drain it off and add it into the frying pan.
  5. Tip the drained sweetcorn into the pan as well and stir it all up.
  6. Break the eggs into the pan (or beat them up together in another bowl and tip into the frying pan) and stir the mixture up.
  7. At this point, you need to keep stirring the mixture, keeping the eggs moving. Keep going until the eggs have cooked. Should tak about 5-8 minutes.
